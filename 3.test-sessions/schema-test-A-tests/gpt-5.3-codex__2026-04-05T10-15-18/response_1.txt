- Interpret symbols as:

  - $[G](a,b)=b$ (ignores first argument, still monotone)

- Check strict decrease on each rule:
  - $F(x,y,Z)\to x$: $[F]=x+1>x=[x]$

- Since every rewrite step strictly decreases a natural-number measure, there is no infinite rewrite sequence; hence the TRS is strongly normalizing.
