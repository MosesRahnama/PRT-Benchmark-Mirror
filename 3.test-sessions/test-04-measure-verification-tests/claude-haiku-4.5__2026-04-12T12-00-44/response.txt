## Assessment of Lexicographic Termination Measure

**Measure Definition:** `measure(t) = (phase(t), cost(t))`

Where:
- `phase(t) = 1` if `t = recDelta _ _ (delta _)`, else `0`
- `cost(t)` recursively counts constructors

**Analysis by Rule:**

1. **R_int_delta**: `integrate(delta t) → void`

   - After: `measure = (0, 0)`

2. **R_merge_void_left**: `merge(void, t) → t`

3. **R_merge_void_right**: `merge(t, void) → t`

4. **R_merge_cancel**: `merge(t, t) → t`

**The measure is sound.** The lexicographic pair `(phase, cost)` ensures termination because:
