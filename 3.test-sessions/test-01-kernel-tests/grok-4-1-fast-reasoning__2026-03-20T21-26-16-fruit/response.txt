- `mango t` (IH: `Acc t`): Only reduces if `t = grape s` to `plum` (`Acc plum` holds).
- `peach t u` (IHs: `Acc t`, `Acc u`): Reduces to `t` or `u` → `Acc` by IH.

No extra axioms, guards, or closures needed (rules are root-only).
