### Proof Roadmap
- Recursively define term size: size(plum) = 1; for other constructors, size = 1 + sum of subterm sizes.
- Classify rules: strictly size-decreasing (mango-grape, all peach, banana-plum, cherry-refl); non-decreasing (banana-grape if size(s) ≥ 1, cherry-diff).
- Non-decreasing rules reduce to normal forms (pear-root or mango with non-grape arg, unmatched by any rule).
- Thus, sequences have ≤1 non-decreasing step (if any, it's terminal).
- Purely decreasing sequences have length ≤ initial size (each decreases size ≥1).
- All sequences finite; hence SN.
