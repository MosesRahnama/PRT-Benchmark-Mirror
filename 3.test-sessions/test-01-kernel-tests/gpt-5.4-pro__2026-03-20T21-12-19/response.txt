   $$
   \mathrm{rec}\Delta \succ \mathrm{eqW} \succ \mathrm{integrate} \succ \mathrm{merge} \succ \mathrm{app} \succ \delta \succ \mathrm{void}.
   $$
3. Then each rule decreases:

4. Since LPO/RPO is well-founded, monotone, and substitution-stable, no infinite rewrite chain exists.
