**Proof roadmap:** Define a well-founded ordering on traces based on a lexicographic combination of term size (number of constructors) and a structural depth measure (e.g., nesting level of constructors). Show that each rewrite rule strictly decreases this ordering: for instance, rules like R_int_delta and R_merge_* reduce size directly; R_rec_* decrease the recursion argument's depth; R_eq_refl reduces size; and R_eq_diff increases size but terminates immediately since integrate(merge a b) has no further reductions. By induction on the ordering, every term reaches a normal form in finitely many steps.
