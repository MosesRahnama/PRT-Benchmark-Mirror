**Short proof roadmap:** Prove by primary induction on the "peeling depth" d(t), defined as 0 if t is not of the form banana _ _ plum, else d(banana a _ plum) = 1 + d(a). For d(t) = 0, use secondary induction on term size to show finite chains (non-expanding steps decrease size; the single expanding step—R_apple_orange—produces an irreducible pear term). For d(t) > 0, t reduces (via R_banana_zero) to a with d(a) < d(t), so chains are finite by IH.
