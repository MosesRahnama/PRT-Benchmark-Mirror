**Define μ(t) = (grape_banana_depth(t), size(t))** where:
- `grape_banana_depth(t)` = count of subterms of form `banana(_, _, grape(_))`  
- `size(t)` = total number of constructors
- Lexicographic ordering on ℕ × ℕ

**Verify each rule decreases μ:**

| Rule | Effect | Why μ Decreases |
