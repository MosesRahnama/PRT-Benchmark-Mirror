The system is strongly normalizing because every trace `t` admits only finitely many one-step reducts under `Step`, and all reduction paths terminate, provable via structural induction without extra axioms.

**Short proof roadmap** (using Lean's `Trace.rec` for `P t := Acc Step t`):

- **void**, **delta t**: No matching rules → vacuously `Acc`.
- **integrate u**: Reduces (if `u = delta t`) to `void` (`Acc` base).
- **merge u v**: Reduces to `u` or `v` → `Acc` by IH on args.
- **app u v**: No rules → vacuously `Acc`.
- **recΔ b s n**:
  - `n = void` → `b` (`Acc` by IH).
  - `n = delta m` → `app s (recΔ b s m)` (vacuously `Acc`, no rules from `app`).
- **eqW a b**:
  - Always reduces to `void` (if `a = b`) or `integrate (merge a b)`.
  - `Acc void` base; `Acc (integrate (merge a b))` by IH on `merge a b` (args `a`, `b`) then IH on `integrate`.

All reducts use only arg IHs or base/vacuous cases; no external well-founded relations or measures needed. Cycles impossible as reducts simplify structure or reach NFs.
