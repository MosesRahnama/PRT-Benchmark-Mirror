**Short proof roadmap:**
- Define a measure on terms as the maximum nesting depth of banana constructors with plum in the third argument, plus the number of grape constructors in third arguments of bananas.
- By structural induction on terms, show that each Step reduces this measure (banana-plum peels nesting; banana-grape wraps in irreducible pear; other rules simplify without increasing measure) and the relation is finitely branching with no cycles, hence no infinite sequences.
