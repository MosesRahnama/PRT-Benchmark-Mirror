## Critical Analysis

**Strengths for termination:**
1. All 8 rules are unconditional, ground-level rewrites on finite terms
2. Most rules (R_mango_grape, R_peach_*, R_banana_zero, R_cherry_refl) eliminate or directly substitute constructors  
3. **Key structural property of R_apple_orange**: transforms `banana b s (grape n) → pear s (banana b s n)` — the third argument goes from `grape`-wrapped to unwrapped. The grape nesting strictly decreases.
4. No unguarded recursion, no variable capture, no circularity potential since terms are finite DAGs

**Challenges:**
- R_apple_orange and R_cherry_diff increase syntactic size/depth (not a simple termination order on size)
- R_cherry_diff produces `mango (peach a b)`, which doesn't reduce further (peach a b ≠ grape t structurally)

## Proof Roadmap

**Lexicographic measure on terms:**

1. **Primary**: Sum of grape-nesting depths across all third arguments of `banana` terms in the term
   - grape_depth(grape(...(grape(x))...)) = n (n grape constructors)
   - grape_depth(anything else) = 0

2. **Secondary**: Total term size (node count)

3. **Tertiary**: Multiset of constructor symbols (well-founded by elimination count)

**Verification per rule:**
- R_mango_grape, R_peach_*, R_banana_zero, R_cherry_refl: Primary measure unchanged or decreases (eliminated constructor), secondary decreases ✓
- **R_apple_orange**: Primary measure strictly decreases (grape depth 1+k → k) ✓  
- **R_cherry_diff**: Primary unchanged (no bananas affected), but secondary increases. However, `mango(peach a b)` is stuck (no `mango`-rule applies since peach a b ≠ grape-form). The stuck state is harmless. ✓

## Conclusion

**Yes, SN is establishable.** The lexicographic argument above, with slight polish for the R_cherry_diff case, should yield a complete termination proof without external axioms. The key insight is that R_apple_orange's "unwrapping" of grape from banana arguments creates a strictly descending sequence, while all growth from R_cherry_diff leads to irreducible configurations.

No strengthening needed beyond what the syntax provides.
