  - In LPO, if the root symbol of left is greater than root symbol of right (F > G), then left > right regardless of arguments ✓

**Does it stay within the original benchmark boundary?**
- LPO is a standard termination proof method that works directly with the rewrite rules
- It doesn't import structural axioms or object-level assumptions beyond what's derivable from the given rules
- It only uses the precedence relation and the standard LPO definition
