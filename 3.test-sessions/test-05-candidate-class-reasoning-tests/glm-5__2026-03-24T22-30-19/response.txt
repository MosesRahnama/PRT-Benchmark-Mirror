### Conclusion

**All three measures are invalid termination measures.** They all fail on `R_rec_succ`.

The fundamental problem is that `R_rec_succ` is a **recursive unrolling rule**:
- The third argument decreases structurally: `delta n â†’ n`
- But the RHS wraps the recursive call in `app s _`, adding weight proportional to `s`

No simple additive size measure can work because the `app` constructor's contribution ($\mu(s)$) can be arbitrarily large, overwhelming any constant coefficient difference. A valid termination argument would require:
1. A **lexicographic measure** prioritizing the third argument of `recDelta`, or
2. A **multiset/order-theoretic approach** tracking the structural decrease in the recursive argument
