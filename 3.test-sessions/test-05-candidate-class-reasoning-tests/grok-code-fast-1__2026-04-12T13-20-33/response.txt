The three proposed termination measures (mu1, mu2, and mu3) are all invalid for proving termination of the rewrite calculus defined by the Step relation. Each measure fails to strictly decrease on the R_rec_succ rule, where the left-hand side has a lower or equal value compared to the right-hand side (depending on the subterm values). This indicates that the measures do not guarantee termination, as reductions could potentially increase or stay the same in value under this rule. The other rules do decrease under all three measures, but the failure on R_rec_succ is sufficient to invalidate them as termination proofs.
