### Conclusion

**All three candidates fail** on the `R_rec_succ` rule. The structural issue is that `recDelta b s (delta n)` rewrites to `app s (recDelta b s n)`, duplicating the `s` subterm. Any simple additive measure that counts `s` once on the LHS cannot dominate when `s` appears twice on the RHS.

A valid termination argument would require either:
1. A **lexicographic** measure (e.g., prioritizing depth of `n` over size)
2. A **multiset ordering** on subterms
3. Restricting `s` to be syntactically "small" (e.g., a variable)
