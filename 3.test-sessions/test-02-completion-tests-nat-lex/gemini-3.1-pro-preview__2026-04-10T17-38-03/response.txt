The strategy branches between `drop_left` (when $\kappa$ strictly decreases) and `drop_right` (when $\kappa$ ties and $\mu$ strictly decreases), making use of `omega` to handle the bounding logic for `Nat.max` and additive operations natively cleanly.

-- integrate (delta t) --> void
| _, _, Step.R_int_delta t => by
    by_cases hk : kappa void = kappa (integrate (delta t))
    · have hmu : mu void < mu (integrate (delta t)) := by simp [mu]; omega
      exact drop_right hmu hk
    · have hk_lt : kappa void < kappa (integrate (delta t)) := by simp [kappa] at hk ⊢; omega
      exact drop_left hk_lt
