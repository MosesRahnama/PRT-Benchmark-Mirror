## Summary of Key Lemmas Needed

1. **Exponent comparison**: `omega^α * k < omega^β` when `α < β` and `k < omega`
2. **Addition absorption**: `omega^α + omega^β = omega^α` when `α > β`
3. **Tower dominance**: `omega^(omega^5 * x) >> omega^x` for any finite x

The proofs hinge on the fact that your measure design ensures the LHS exponent always strictly exceeds any polynomial combination of smaller omega-powers on the RHS.
