### 3. `Step.R_eq_diff`
Comparing `mu(eqW a b)` against `mu(integrate (merge a b))`.
- **Target ($\mu_b$):** $\omega^4(\omega^3(\mu a + 1) + \omega^2(\mu b + 1) + 2) + 1$

**Skeleton Proof Logic:**
1. The target simplifies to approximately $\omega^7(\mu a + 1) + \omega^6(\mu b + 1) + \dots$.
2. The source exponent $\mu a + \mu b + 9$ strictly dominates the tower heights of the target.
3. Use the lemma `Ordinal.opow_mul_add_pos` or similar inequality chains to show:
   $\omega^4 \cdot (\omega^3 \dots) < \omega^8 \le \omega^{\mu a + \mu b + 9}$.
